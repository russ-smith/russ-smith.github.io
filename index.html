About me

Hi. I'm Russ Smith. Welcome to my page.

I'm a programmer with an interest in games, graphics, audio, and all things mathematical and multimedia-based.

I'm currently working at startup Zulleon in Dublin, helping to build the OOKs brand for kids. While here, I've helped build and ship a collection of mobile games, and update and maintain our backend selling merch based on user-generated content.

Before that, I spent two years living in Shanghai, where I worked at a young, mid-sized indie games company. I worked mainly on graphics-related tasks for our main project, a networked racing game, but I was also responsible for prototyping various side projects, none of which saw the light of day unfortunately.

For my last year there, I also taught programming to Chinese kids in weekend and summer classes, using Python to demonstrate the basic concepts and create simple projects, including clones of a few classic games.

On this site, you can find some examples of my previous work, some personal projects I've been working on, and links to my email, C.V. and other stuff around the web.
